## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not approve unknown “collect requests”
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Always verify the identity of the sender before making payments
- Use official apps only for transactions
- Enable app lock and two-factor authentication
- Regularly check bank transaction history
- Do not scan random QR codes from unknown sources
- Be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true
- Report fraud immediately on https://cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
